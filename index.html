Work History

Two common threads that have run throughout my career are creativity and technology, they are qualities that I both look for and aim to bring to any role. For me programming was a logical next-step, incorporating my enthusiasm for technology and my passion for creativity.

Iron Oak Media Director / Producer

Running my own company has given me the skills and experience necessary to drive and lead projects, as well as to manage client relationships and expectations.

I have offered a range of services and ‘packages’ that range from single consultations to entire marketing campaigns. I develop a core concept or theme with the client, and work with them to build that out into a fully formed idea and project.

As well as producing videos and audio recordings I host workshops, build strategy plans and help the client to develop their brand.

Spotify
IT & AV Engineer

Working within IT allowed me to develop a number of skills outside my core role as an AV Engineer, working as an administrator with systems like the Atlassian suite & Google’s administrator console. As the person spearheading the installation of Google hangouts across the company, as well as into a live stage environment, I have presented our integration solutions to the Google Hangouts department and spoken at one of their conferences.

As part of improving the security of the company’s video content I commissioned a development team to build an internal video portal. During the project I worked closely with them to design the site, and ensure that the most stringent of security protocols were adhered to.

My role also encompassed designing technical schematics for AV systems within new offices and stages. I would work with office co-ordinators and stakeholders to establish needs, source and negotiate with installation companies, and manage and oversee installations.

In the core Audio & Video role I was running IPTV broadcasting events, recording podcasts in the sound recording studio and filming video content.

University of Portsmouth
AV Loan Store Technician

My role was varied, and provided a lot of opportunity for learning and exploration within my field. I was part of a team providing front line technical support for camera systems, as well as managing the front desk of the University loan store, and administering the equipment database and booking systems.

Our team were responsible for the supervision, maintenance and distribution of over 1.5 million pounds of professional AV equipment. I had many opportunities to use this equipment while producing recordings from talks and events which took place at the university, and while training other members of staff.

Nationwide Building Society
Internal Tech Support

I provided technical support for other employees within the business, as well as some direct customer support via phone. Our team focussed on understanding and troubleshooting financial systems and how they integrated, to gain a deep understanding of potential problems and how to resolve them.

During my time I worked hard to document systems and common issues, building comprehensive documentation to drastically reduce the on-boarding time for new hires and improve the speed and reliability of the support we provided to the buisness.
